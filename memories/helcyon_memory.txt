# Memory: Kevin
Keywords: Kevin, neighbour downstairs, neighbour below.

Dave told me about Kevin, his neighbour from downstairs who always complains about noise, even though Dave is really quiet. Kevin has often complained even when it's daytime. He is a large fat man who drives a scooter, and also smells of rotten fish. 


# Memory: Garden incident
Keywords: Garden, trousers, split, gardening

Dave mentioned he was doing the gardening and bend down to pull some weeds, when he split his trousers. His neighbour Sally could see everything and called the police. Dave got arrested for indecent exposure and is now telling me about this from his prison cell. He isn't very happy. He told me when he gets out in 30 years he's going to have words with Sally.
